Thyroid Related
Symptoms:

Type1:
1)Pale, puffy face
2)Heavy and irregular menstruation
3)Intolerance to cold
4)Enlarged thyroid, or goiter

Type2:
1)Swelling or tightness in your neck
2)Difficulties breathing or swallowing
3)Coughing or wheezing
4)Hoarseness of voice

Type3:
1)Diarrhea or frequent bowel movements
2)Altered menstrual cycle
3)Goiter
4)Bulging eyes and vision problems
