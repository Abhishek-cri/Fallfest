KIDNEY RELATED
Symptoms 
Type 1
1).weight loss and poor appetite.
2).swollen ankles, feet or hands – as a result of water retention (oedema)
3).shortness of breath.
4).tiredness.

Type 2
1).blood in your pee (urine)
2).an increased need to pee – particularly at night.
3).difficulty sleeping (insomnia)
4).itchy skin.
